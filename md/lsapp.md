#### lsapp(显示app列表) 使用方法:
````
lsapp -devi string

example:

1. fd lsapp

2. fd lsapp -devi local
````

#### local app is emtry

#### -devi:
- default: usb
- -devi usb(usb devi)
- -devi u(usb devi)
- -devi local(local devi)
- -devi localhost(local devi)
- -devi ip:port(remote device)
- -devi 1234(devi id)
